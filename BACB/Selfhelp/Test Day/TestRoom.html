What You Can Bring into the Testing Room

  • Items allowed in the testing room
  • Test center storage for personal items

Items allowed in the testing room

For BACB exams administered at Pearson VUE test centers, you are only allowed to bring the following items into the testing room:

  • One erasable noteboard and marker, which will be provided by the test center.
  • Built-in calculators, if your exam allows calculator use (no personal calculators are permitted).

Note: No personal items, such as mobile phones, watches, wallets, or bags, are allowed in the testing room. These items must be stored in the designated lockers provided by the test center.

Test center storage for personal items

All personal items must be stored in a locker provided at the test center. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Mobile phones, smartwatches, and other electronic devices
  • Wallets, purses, and bags
  • Food and drinks (except for medical needs with prior approval)
  • Outerwear such as jackets or hats

Personal items must remain in the locker for the entire duration of the exam, including during breaks. Accessing personal items during an unscheduled break or tampering with locker contents may lead to the termination of the exam.
