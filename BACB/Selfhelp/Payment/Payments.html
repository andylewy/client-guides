What forms of payment can I use?

Pearson VUE collects payments on behalf of the BACB for all exam-related fees. The following payment methods are accepted:

  • Credit and debit cards: All major credit and debit cards are accepted.
  • Vouchers: Vouchers can be purchased from the Pearson VUE Voucher Store and redeemed at checkout.

Note: Make sure that your payment method is valid at the time of the transaction, as the system will not process expired or invalid cards.

Paying with a voucher

When you schedule your exam, select Exam Voucher as your form of payment. For expired vouchers and other voucher issues, please contact Pearson VUE Customer Service

Test receipts

After payment is successfully processed, you will receive a confirmation email from Pearson VUE. This email will include the payment receipt, confirming the transaction. You can also access your test receipt by logging into your Pearson VUE account.

For any discrepancies in your receipt, please contact Pearson VUE Customer Service.

Refunds

Refunds are provided based on when the cancellation is made:

  • More than 30 days before the appointment: Full refund.
  • Between 48 hours to 30 days before the appointment: Partial refund.
  • Less than 48 hours before the appointment: No refund is available.

Refunds are generally issued to the original payment method used at the time of purchase.

Where is my refund?

Refunds typically take 3-10 business days to process. However, the exact timeline may vary depending on your bank or payment provider. If you do not see your refund after 10 business days, contact Pearson VUE Customer Service for further assistance.
